Co-Share

If You're Admitted, You Pay Just a Small Share

When you're admitted as an inpatient, you pay a small co-share portion of the approved bill — and the community shares the rest. Your co-share is based on your age group and is always capped.

Age GroupCo-Share Rate
Age 0 – 525%
Age 6 – 4510%
Age 46 and above20%
  • Co-share is capped at RM500 per admission.
  • No co-share applies for admissions to government hospitals.
  • Loyal members (3+ years, no inpatient claims) can claim a co-share reimbursement up to RM500.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Medical Cost-Sharing is a community-based model where members voluntarily pool resources to help cover each other's approved medical expenses. When a member has an eligible medical bill, the community shares the cost among all active members, making healthcare more accessible and affordable.

We Kongsi is NOT an insurance product. Unlike insurance, We Kongsi is a community-driven program where members help members — there's no insurance company taking profit. Members contribute only when there are approved medical cases, making it more transparent and community-focused.

Your monthly sharing depends on the approved medical cases that month, divided among all active members. Inpatient sharing is capped at RM60 per member. With the COB option you may contribute an additional RM15. The membership fee is separate and fixed at RM40/month (or RM100/quarter, RM360/year).

If there are no approved hospital cases in a month, no inpatient sharing is required. Your Sharing Account balance is carried forward and remains available for future months when the community needs support.

COB provides support for everyday illnesses, including telemedicine via HEYDOC Health and clinic visits at MiCare panel clinics nationwide. Members receive up to RM120 support per episode, for up to 8 episodes per year, covering 17 eligible outpatient conditions.

Yes, COB is optional. You can choose an Inpatient-Only package without COB, or include COB for comprehensive outpatient support. Speak to your We Kongsi agent for details on the available options.

Each month, all approved medical bills are totalled and divided equally among active members. For example, if RM200,000 in bills is approved across 5,000 active members, each member shares RM40 that month. Inpatient sharing is always capped at RM60/month.
